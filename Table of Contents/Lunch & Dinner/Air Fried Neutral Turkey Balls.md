## Ingredients
- 450 g ground turkey
- 1 large egg
- 28 g panko breadcrumbs
- 1 tsp minced garlic
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 1/2 tsp salt

## Directions
1. Mix ingredients together, form into meatballs. 
2. Air fry at 400 for 16-18 minutes.

## Notes
Easy to pair with veggies and carbs
